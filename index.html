Fun facts about Harley-Davidson

  • Harley-Davidson was founded in a small wooden shed in Milwaukee in 1903.
  • During World War I and II, Harley-Davidson produced motorcycles for the military.
  • The iconic "potato-potato" engine sound is trademarked.
  • The term "Hog" comes from a 1920s racing team that had a pet pig as their mascot.
  • Harley-Davidson has built bicycles, golf carts, and even snowmobiles in the past.
  • Some Harley engines, like the V-Rod, were developed in collaboration with Porsche.
  • The most expensive Harley, the *Cosmic Starship*, sold for $3 million.
  • A Harley-Davidson motorcycle washed up in Canada after the 2011 Japan tsunami.
  • Harley-Davidson bikes have been featured in movies like *Easy Rider* and *Terminator 2*.
